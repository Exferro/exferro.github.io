Hi, I am Aleksei!

I am a second year Master student at Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology. I am interested in science — in quantum computation and quantum information in particular — and I want to be a scientist!

Since June 2018, I am a research assistant at Quantum Communications Group of Russian Quantum Center. My research lies in the field of postprocessing for Quantum Key Distribution. Two years before that I spent conducting research on radio resource scheduling for 5G networks with Institute for Information Transmission Problems.

This year I finish my Master program and I am striving to start a PhD on quantum computation and quantum information. I believe that this field is to bring us insights on the fundamental properties of Universe — and useful practical applications as well. Thus, I hope to contribute to the future era, when people do not "Shut up and calculate" as David Merman said, but use quantum theory in its full.

What is the purpose of the site

Well, initially I considered it to be some kind of portfolio — more vibrant than CV, but not too much. However, during the work, I remembered how once I spent eight hours in the visa center of Spain trying to obtain my passport — I had flight to Istanbul next day to attend a conference and present my paper. There I get acquainted with a man who was waiting for his passport as well. He told me: "In the end, we have nothing but the stories we've participated in and the stories we've heard about. So, tell me yours and I'll tell you mine".

Hence, I decided to not only list here my achievements and illustrate them, but to tell a little story about each one I treat to be worth mentioning. I am aware of great amount of work this decision entails — probably, it even contradicts my initial aim — but I simply want these stories to be told.

Hence, not that fast, I am working on this site — don't be scared if some sections are empty — I'll try to fill them as soon as possible.

What can be found here

At the moment, I am going to follow the next structure:

  • Research — there I tell stories about research which is my main activity. Projects are given in the reverse chronological order. SIC-POVMs, QKD, URLLC for 5G — so many abbreviations, what do they mean?!
  • Projects— unsurprusingly, there are stories about projects I would like to tell! Pss, there is some digital signal processing, linear programming and even blockchain!
  • Hobbies — third in a row, a section about my interests! Radio, Gogol-School and playing bass! (Ah, yet another intrigue!)
  • Where the Story Begins — it is just a little story about myself. I tried to explain how I became fascinated with Physics, Math and Computer science and how I decided to enroll to MIPT. (sometimes I suspect that I am becoming sentimental)
  • Publications — the most boring section! I decided that at personal site there should be a list of publications — so I simply added it. That simple!

Please, note that site is currently now in the active development. Hence, if some sections are empty, don't be angry, just visit it later!
