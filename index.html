Hi, I am Aleksei!

I am a second year Master student at Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology. As well, I am a research assistant at Quantum Communications Group of Russian Quantum Center, my research lies in the field of postprocessing for Quantum Key Distribution.

Since school ages I am fascinated with Physics, Math and Computer Science — I studied them hard, I participated in olympiades and I entered MIPT to support my interest and to have them as my lifetime occupation.

What I am keen on the most at the moment, it is quantum computing and quantum information — for I treat this field to be a one which will heavily influence the shape of our world in the nearest future. Moreover, I highly appreciate how comprehensive quantum computing is — I may not restrict myself to some particular topic — coming up with a novel qubit realization interests me just the same as relating BQP to NP! Of course, I dream of becoming a quantum information scientist after finishing Master studies!

Purpose of the site

Well, initially I considered it to be some kind of portfolio — more vibrant than CV, but not too much. However, I remembered how once I spent eight hours in the visa center of Spain trying to obtain my passport. There, a man, who was also waiting for his passport, and whom I hardly knew, told me: "In the end, we have nothing but the stories we've participated in and the stories we've heard about. So, tell me yours and I'll tell you mine".

Hence, I decided to not only list here my achievements and add some images, but to tell a little story about each one I treat to be worth mentioning (probably, if adding images to CV was conventional, there wouldn't be this cite...). I am aware of great amount of work this decision entails — probably, it even contradicts my initial aim — but I simply want these stories to be told.

And now, not so fast, I am working on this site — don't be scared if some sections are empty — I'll try to fill them as soon as possible! :-)
