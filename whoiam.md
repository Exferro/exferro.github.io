It was Saturday and my eighth year at school had just began. I was going to the first lesson of elective on Physics. Month before that I had started visiting sailing lessons and thus I wasn't really in the mood — of course, I would have preferred to spend another Septemeber evening with staysails and spinnakers! Moreover, I had already visited several lessons of Math elective and they weren't a pleasure trip, not in a tiny bit — they were bringing my mind to new levels with the price of headache. 

Nevertheless, my Mom insisted that I had attended at least first lesson on Physics, just to compare. I obeyed, what else I could have done.

That changed **everything** (Mom, thank you!).
